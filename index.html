Welcome Message

Welcome to Rivers State College of Nursing Sciences, Port Harcourt! We are dedicated to training the next generation of healthcare professionals with the highest standards of nursing education, clinical practice, and research. Our institution fosters academic excellence, innovation, and compassionate care in healthcare delivery.

The Rivers State College of Nursing Sciences, Port Harcourt, was established to bridge the gap in quality healthcare education. With a legacy of excellence, the college has produced outstanding nurses and midwives serving in various healthcare institutions nationwide.

More About Us

Our Vision

  • The vision of the College is to be centre of excellence in Nursing Education.
  • To train competent and polyvalent nurses that are research oriented, and nurses that function independently, and in collaboration with other members of the health team and related sectors in planning, delivering and evaluating health care services.
  • Be ready to conform to new changes in nursing education.

Our Mission

To provide a standardized educational programme that will enable the production of competent nursing graduates who can provide quality health care services and are capable of functioning in any area of the health management system in Nigeria and the diaspora.
